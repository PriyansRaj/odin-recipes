Easy pancakes

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower
  • Lemon wedges
  • Caster sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare Batter: Mix 100g plain flour, 2 eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp oil, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  2. Rest (Optional): Let the batter rest for 30 mins for better texture.
  3. Heat Pan: Warm a frying or crÃªpe pan over medium heat, lightly oil it.
  4. Cook Pancakes: Pour half a ladle of batter per pancake, cook for 1 min each side until golden brown.
  5. Keep Warm: Store cooked pancakes in a low oven to keep warm.
  6. Serve: Enjoy with lemon and sugar, or your favorite fillings.
  7. Store Leftovers: Cool pancakes, layer with parchment, wrap in cling film, refrigerate.
